Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking:10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional fillings: cheese, herbs, vegetables

Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs:
    In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat the pan:
    Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    3. Cook the omelette:
    Pour the eggs into the skillet and cook for about 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to set.
    4. Add filling:
    Add your desired fillings on one half of the omelette.
    5. Fold and serve:
    Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute until fully set.
    6. Enjoy:
    Serve hot and enjoy!
